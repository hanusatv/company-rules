# Company AL Naming Conventions

Our company standards for AL object naming...

## Tables
- Prefix: `HTV_` (Company)
- Format: `HTV_<Object Name>`
- Example: `HTV_CustomerOrder`

## Codeunits
- Suffix with purpose: `Mgt`, `Handler`, `Integration`
- Example: `CMP Order Mgt.`
